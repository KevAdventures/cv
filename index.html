Stoic Elk

Pasture raised grass-fed grass-finished beef and beef liver jerky. Buy some here

We love animals, and want to support the environment, but we also love eating meat. Turns out, you can love and support all those things at the same time. All our meat is sourced from regenerative farms that raise their animals as they should, on rotating pastures that bring biodiversity and nutrients back into the soil, benefitting the health of the animals, as well as the entire ecosystem. It also means when you eat our product all these added nutrients are getting passed on to you.
It’s a win-win-win.
We also believe in the benefits of including organ meat into diets. Organ meats are not very popular, and either end up being tossed or just used in pet food, but they are some of the most nutritionally dense foods on the planet. Unfortunately, the look and taste of organs can be quite off putting for many people. That’s why all our jerky is a specifically proportioned blend of meat and liver. So, you can get all the nutritional benefits without sacrificing taste.
